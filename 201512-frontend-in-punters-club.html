Challenge 1

Brand new look and feel

Solution

Solution:

  • Brand new Sass library from scratch
  • Fully portable (modules, mixins, variable settings)
  • Encapsulated in #punters namespace

Challenge 2

Responsive to Products

to deliver different user experiences (functions and forms) for different products on different platforms

Solution

Solution:

  • JS driven product/platform query
    (e.g. 'touch mobile ios iphone iosnative' on iPhone native app)
  • Let CSS does the hard work with a touch of JS on view logics
